Connect Apple Calendar

Export your calendar as a file, then import it into FitSync.

  1. Open Calendar on your Mac.
  2. In the menu bar click File → Export → Export…
  3. Choose a save location and click Export. A .ics file is saved.
  4. Click the button below and select that file.
  1. On your iPhone / iPad open Settings → Calendar → Accounts.
  2. Tap your iCloud account → iCloud.com and sign in on a browser, then follow the iCloud.com tab steps.
  3. Alternatively, share a single calendar: open the Calendar app → tap the calendar name → Share Calendar → choose View-Only link and open it — this downloads an .ics feed.
  4. Save the file, then click the button below to import it.
  1. Go to icloud.com/calendar and sign in.
  2. Click the share icon (broadcast waves) next to a calendar in the left sidebar.
  3. Enable Public Calendar and copy the link shown.
  4. Paste the link into your browser address bar, change webcal:// to https://, and press Enter — your browser will download the .ics file.
  5. Click the button below and select that file.
